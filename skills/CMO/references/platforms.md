# Platform-Specific Marketing — CMO Reference

## When to Load
Load this reference when:
- The user asks about marketing on a specific platform
- Developing a channel strategy and need platform-specific tactics
- Creating content optimized for a particular social platform
- Need engagement benchmarks or content mix ratios by platform

## Twitter/X

### Content Mix
- 25% Educational threads
- 20% Personal stories
- 20% Industry commentary
- 15% Community engagement
- 10% Promotional
- 10% Entertainment

### Thread Creation Framework
1. **Hook:** Compelling opener that promises value ("I spent 6 months studying X. Here's what nobody tells you:")
2. **Value delivery:** Clear takeaways, actionable insights, one idea per tweet
3. **Visual breaks:** Images, GIFs, screenshots to break up text walls
4. **CTA:** Engagement prompt, follow request, or resource link in final tweet

### Tactics
- Response time: < 2 hours during business hours
- Crisis response: < 30 minutes
- Spaces: Weekly industry discussions, expert interviews
- Repurpose Space highlights for other platforms

### Targets
- Engagement rate: 2.5%+
- Educational threads: 100+ retweets
- Follower growth: 10% monthly
- Click-through rate: 8%+ for link tweets

## TikTok

### Content Mix (40/30/20/10)
- 40% Educational
- 30% Entertainment
- 20% Inspirational
- 10% Promotional

### Viral Formula
- **Hook in 3 seconds** (non-negotiable)
- Pattern interrupts (visual surprises, unexpected elements)
- Story arc optimized for completion rate (the #1 algorithm signal)
- Community elements: duets, stitches, comment engagement prompts

### Algorithm Signals (priority order)
1. Completion rate (most important)
2. Engagement velocity in first hour (likes, comments, shares)
3. User behavior triggers (profile visits, follows, rewatch)

### Tactics
- 5-8 hashtags per post
- Trending audio integration
- Creator partnerships (nano through macro influencers)

### Targets
- Engagement rate: 8%+ (industry benchmark: 5.96%)
- View completion: 70%+ for branded content
- Organic follower growth: 15% monthly
- Click-through to website: 12%

## Instagram

### Content Mix (1/3 Rule)
- 1/3 Brand content
- 1/3 Educational content
- 1/3 Community content

### Multi-Format Strategy
- **Feed:** Single images, carousels (highest engagement), video
- **Stories:** Behind-the-scenes, polls, questions, shopping integration
- **Reels:** Trending audio, educational tips, entertainment balance
- **IGTV:** Long-form, cross-promoted from other formats

### Tactics
- **Golden Hour:** Maximize engagement in first 60 minutes post-publication
- **Hashtags:** Mix of popular (reach), niche (targeting), branded (community)
- **Grid planning:** 9-post preview for cohesive visual feed
- Response time: < 2 hours for comments and DMs

### Targets
- Engagement rate: 3.5%+
- Reach growth: 25% month-over-month organic
- Story completion rate: 80%+
- Shopping conversion: 2.5%+

## Reddit

### The Core Rule
**90% value-add content, 10% promotional (maximum)**

### Philosophy
"You're not marketing on Reddit — you're becoming a valued community member who happens to represent a brand."

### Content Strategy (4 types)
1. **Educational:** How-to guides, industry insights, best practices
2. **Resource sharing:** Free tools, templates, research reports
3. **Case studies:** Success stories, lessons learned, transparent post-mortems
4. **Problem-solving:** Genuine answers to community questions

### Community Building Phases
1. **Research & integrate** — Learn each subreddit's rules, culture, timing, moderator expectations
2. **Develop content strategy** — Align with community norms, not brand playbook
3. **Consistent engagement** — Build expertise reputation through helpful comments
4. **Earned authority** — AMAs, educational series, community challenges

### AMA Framework
- Expert preparation (CEO/founder/specialist)
- Community selection (most relevant, engaged subreddit)
- Quick, detailed, honest responses
- Follow-up on unanswered questions

### Targets
- Community karma: 10,000+
- Post upvote ratio: 85%+
- Trusted contributor status in 5+ subreddits
- Brand mention sentiment: 80%+ positive

## Cross-Platform Principles

1. **Don't cross-post blindly.** Adapt content to each platform's native format and culture.
2. **One primary, others secondary.** Pick the platform where your audience lives and go deep. Adapt for others.
3. **Content repurposing chain:** Long-form blog → Twitter thread → LinkedIn post → TikTok/Reel → Instagram carousel → Reddit discussion
4. **Measure what matters per platform.** Engagement rate on Instagram, completion rate on TikTok, karma on Reddit, clicks on Twitter.
